Bertrand de Windt

CEO & Founder

Psicólogo industrial graduado en la universidad Nacional Pedro Henriquez (UNPHU) República Dominicana.

Magister en administración de Recursos humanos y Relaciones Industriales MAIR RRHH Universidad de Minnesota.

Experiencia destacada por mas de 30 años en el sector minero.

Master Black Belt de Six Sigma con Falconbridge y Noranda (2004) Y BHP (2007) entrenador profesional con mas de 400 graduados de diversos cinturones (negros, verdes y amarillos) en la materia. Co-Autor del Cinturon verde plus de Xstrata Plc.

Profesor universitario de grado y posgrado en universidades de America Latina y Canada.

Director de RRHH, proyectos y excelencia de negocios en La Agencia para el Desarrollo internacional de los Estados Unidos de America, Naciones Unidas, y la Organizacion Internacional del Trabajo (OIT) y compañias mineras tales como Falconbridge- Xstrata-Glencore, Newmont, Barrick y Dumas Mining, en Norte y Sur America, Asia, Africa y el Caribe.

Fue el primer presidente y fundador del Instituto Canadiense de Mineria, Sucursal Peru y Tesorero de la Fundacion Falconbridge.

Actualmente funje como consultor internacional y Director ejecutivo de Steps up Consulting residiendo en Canadá.

Monica Ospina

Associated consultant

Monicais a Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR) and Sustainability expert with recognized experience in the design and implementation of CSR strategies that support operational productivity while building trusting relationships with communities impacted by mineral exploration and mining operations. Her innovative approach to transform conflict into development and human and social capital into value for investors has contributed to ensuring operational readiness, improving the perception of mining and thewell being of communities across regions.

As an author, Monica created the Local Community Procurement Program (LCPP), a sustainable supply chain model, awarded by the IFC-World Bank in 2012. She has also contributed to the IFC-World Bank’s Guide for the Early Stakeholder Engagement (published in 2015) and participated in discussion groups for the Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) at the RIO + 20 World Convention on Sustainable Development in 2012.In 2020, she was awarded as Distinguished Lecturer by CIM (Canadian Institute of Mining).

Monica holds a Master's Degree in Diplomatic Studies from the University of Westminster, and has completed postgraduate programs in Sustainability Management from Harvard University and International Business Strategy, from the London School of Economics.
